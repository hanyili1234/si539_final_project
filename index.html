Four Museums, One City

This site shares what stood out when I visited the four musuems in New York City: Whitney Museum of American Art, the Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, and the Museum of Modern Art. Each page is a short personal reviews of each musuems and things I like about these musuems, also with some tips for first-time visitors.
